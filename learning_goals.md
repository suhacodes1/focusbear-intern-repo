# Learning Goals Reflection

## What do you hope to learn from this internship?

I hope to gain practical, real-world experience working on a product and understand how a development team operates in a professional environment. I want to learn how features are planned, built, tested, and improved over time, and how different tools like Git, issue tracking, and agile workflows are used in practice.

## What skills are you most excited to develop?

I am most excited to improve my data analysis skills using Python, pandas, and SQL, as well as learning how to interpret product data to make meaningful decisions. I also want to strengthen my problem-solving skills and get better at working with real datasets instead of just academic examples.

## What do you think you can contribute to the team?

I think I can contribute by being reliable, willing to learn, and putting in consistent effort. I am comfortable with programming and have experience with Python and basic data analysis, so I can support tasks involving data cleaning, analysis, and documentation. I also try to communicate clearly and ask questions when needed, which helps me learn faster and avoid mistakes.

## What is one area you feel less confident in that you’d like to improve?

One area I would like to improve is my confidence in working with large datasets and more advanced analysis techniques. I sometimes take longer to understand new tools or concepts, so I want to become more efficient and confident when working independently on technical tasks.

## What does success in this internship look like for you?

Success for me would mean completing tasks independently, contributing meaningful work to the team, and improving my technical and professional skills. I would also like to feel more confident in my abilities by the end of the internship and have a better understanding of how data analytics is applied in real-world products.
