# Professionalism at Focus Bear

## Research & Learn

### 1. Professional vs Unprofessional Behaviour in a Casual Work Environment

**Professional behaviour:**

- Communicating clearly and respectfully in messages and meetings
- Being punctual and prepared for meetings
- Taking accountability for tasks and deadlines
- Actively listening to teammates and valuing their input
- Maintaining a positive and collaborative attitude

**Unprofessional behaviour:**

- Ignoring messages or failing to respond in a timely manner
- Interrupting others or not allowing them to speak
- Using inappropriate or disrespectful language
- Missing deadlines without communication
- Dismissing feedback or reacting defensively

---

### 2. Respectful Communication in Remote Teams

- Use clear and concise language to avoid misunderstandings
- Maintain a polite and neutral tone in written communication
- Acknowledge messages and respond within a reasonable timeframe
- Be mindful that tone can be misinterpreted in text
- Approach disagreements constructively and respectfully
- Assume positive intent and seek clarification before reacting

---

### 3. Giving and Receiving Feedback Professionally

**Giving feedback:**

- Focus on actions or outcomes, not the individual
- Be specific and constructive
- Provide suggestions for improvement
- Deliver feedback respectfully and at an appropriate time

**Receiving feedback:**

- Listen actively without interrupting
- Avoid taking feedback personally
- Ask questions to clarify expectations
- Use feedback as an opportunity for growth

---

## Reflection

### 1. Experience with Teamwork and Professionalism

I have experienced effective teamwork in both academic and work environments where communication was clear, responsibilities were well-defined, and team members supported each other. What made it effective was mutual respect, accountability, and openness to feedback.

---

### 2. Steps to Ensure Clear, Respectful, and Inclusive Communication

- Use structured and concise messages
- Avoid assumptions and ask for clarification when needed
- Be mindful of tone and language
- Ensure all team members feel heard and included
- Respond to messages in a timely manner

---

### 3. Contributing to a Positive and Professional Environment

- Maintain a respectful and supportive attitude
- Encourage collaboration and teamwork
- Be approachable and open to different perspectives
- Balance professionalism with a positive and friendly attitude

---

### 4. Preparing Effectively for Meetings

- Review the agenda and relevant materials beforehand
- Prepare questions or updates in advance
- Join meetings on time
- Take notes and follow up on action items

---

### 5. Being Proactive During the Internship

- Take initiative in completing tasks and seeking new opportunities
- Ask questions when unsure rather than waiting
- Keep supervisors updated on progress
- Identify areas for improvement and suggest solutions

---

### 6. Following Up and Escalation

If I need information or action from colleagues or supervisors:

- Send a clear and polite follow-up message
- Provide context and restate the request
- Allow reasonable time for a response

If there is no response:

- Send a second follow-up with a clear deadline
- Escalate to a supervisor if the issue is blocking progress

Escalation would be appropriate when:

- Work is significantly delayed
- There is a lack of communication affecting outcomes
- There are concerns about professional conduct

---

### 7. Things I Will Not Do

- Engage in disrespectful or inappropriate behaviour
- Ignore messages or responsibilities
- Miss deadlines without communication
- React defensively to feedback
- Contribute to a negative or unprofessional work environment

---

## Summary

Professionalism at Focus Bear involves maintaining respectful communication, accountability, and a positive attitude while working in a relaxed and collaborative environment. By being proactive, responsive, and open to feedback, I can contribute effectively to the team and support a healthy work culture.
