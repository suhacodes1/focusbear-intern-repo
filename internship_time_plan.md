# Internship Time Plan

## Overview

This time plan outlines how I will consistently meet the required internship hours by allocating structured work periods each week. The plan is designed to balance my university commitments while ensuring steady progress and accountability.

---

## Weekly Schedule

### Monday

- Time: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM (4 hours)
- Location: Home / Study Space
- Focus: Development tasks, assigned issues, coding

### Tuesday

- Time: 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM (4 hours)
- Location: Home / University Library
- Focus: Meetings, collaboration, documentation

### Wednesday

- Time: 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM (3 hours)
- Location: Home
- Focus: Deep work, feature development

### Thursday

- Time: 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM (4 hours)
- Location: Home / Library
- Focus: Debugging, improvements, testing

### Friday

- Time: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM (4 hours)
- Location: Home
- Focus: Wrap-up tasks, documentation, planning

---

## Total Weekly Hours

- Total: **19 hours per week**

---

## Additional Flexible Hours

To ensure I meet required internship hours, I will also:

- Add **1–3 extra hours on weekends if needed**
- Adjust schedule during lighter university weeks
- Increase hours during project deadlines or busy periods

---

## Time Tracking Plan

I will log all work sessions using Focus Bear by:

- Starting a focus session at the beginning of each work block
- Categorising sessions based on tasks (e.g., coding, meetings, research)
- Maintaining consistent logs as evidence of completed hours

---

## Work Locations

- Primary: Home workspace
- Secondary: University library or quiet study areas

---

## Accountability & Consistency

To ensure I stay on track:

- I will follow a structured weekly routine
- I will review my hours weekly to ensure targets are met
- I will communicate any schedule changes proactively

---

## Summary

This time plan ensures that I consistently meet internship hour requirements while maintaining a balance with my academic responsibilities. By following a structured schedule and tracking my time using Focus Bear, I can ensure accountability, productivity, and a strong learning experience throughout the internship.
